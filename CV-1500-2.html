    CV-1500
    제품설명

    EVIS X1 시스템은 LED 광원과 비디오 프로세서를 통합하여 Olympus가 이전 모델들보다 훨씬 더 컴팩트하고 강력한 플랫폼을 제공합니다. 이 플랫폼은 TXI(Texture & Color Enhancement Imaging), RDI(Red Dichromatic Imaging), BAI-MAC(Brightness Adjustment Imaging with MAintenance of Contrast)와 같은 향상된 관찰 기능을 통해 점막 표면의 구조, 혈관, 그리고 어두운 부위의 대비를 최적화합니다. 또한, 원터치 커넥터(One-Touch Connector)를 사용하여 빠르고 쉬운 연결이 가능하며, 터치 패널의 직관적인 조작과 함께 사용자 편의성을 높여 시술 시간을 단축하도록 설계되었습니다.

    다운로드

    다운로드

    A Unified Platform with 5 LED Spectrum Technology

    LED 광원과 비디오 프로세서를 통합하여, Olympus는 이전 모델들보다 훨씬 더 컴팩트하고 가벼운 강력한 시스템을 개발했습니다

    Broad Compatibility

    CV-1500은 매우 다양한 종류의 내시경에 연결될 수 있어, 광범위한 내시경 지원 기능에 접근할 수 있게 해줍니다.

    Enhanced Observations

    Enhanced Observations
    - TXI (Texture & Color Enhancement Imaging): 점막 표면의 구조, 색상 톤 및 밝기를 최적화합니다.
    - RDI (Red Dichromatic Imaging): 심부 혈관 및 출혈 부위의 시인성을 향상시킵니다.
    - BAI-MAC (Brightness Adjustment Imaging with MAintenance of Contrast): 어두운 부분의 밝기를 (대비를 유지하며) 개선합니다.

    Intuitive, User-friendly Functions

    원터치 커넥터(One-Touch Connector)를 사용하여 빠르고 쉬운 연결이 가능하며, 화이트 밸런스 조정이 필요 없어 워크플로우를 간소화하고 시술 시간을 단축하는 것을 목표로 설정이 단순화되었습니다. 터치 감지 패널은 직관적인 조작을 용이하게 하며, 프리-프리즈(Pre-Freeze) 및 MyCV 모드와 같은 편리한 기능은 사용자 친화적인 작업 환경을 보장합니다. 교체가 필요 없이 수년 동안 지속되는 LED 전구를 사용하여 다운타임(가동 중단 시간)이 줄어듭니다.
    *EVIS EXERA III/EVIS LUCERA ELITE 시리즈 광원 및 프로세서와의 조합 Olympus 1100/1200/1500 시리즈 내시경만 해당

    Power Supply Rated voltage 100-240 V AC; Within ±10%
    Frequency 50/60 Hz; within ±3 Hz
    Rated input 600 VA
    Size Dimensions (W x H x D) 370 x 198 x 488 mm; 398 x 218 x 580 mm (maximum)
    Weight 19.4 kg
    Classification (Medical Electrical Equipment) Type of protection against electric shock Class I
    Degree of protection against electric shock of applied part Depend on applied part. (The degree of protection against electric shock of this product is BF type if the mounting part to be connected to this product is BF type. However CF type is not subject to combination in this product.)
    Degree of protection against explosion The video system center should be kept away from flammable gases.
    Observation Analog signal output VBS composite and Y/C; simultaneous outputs possible.
    Digital signal output 12G-SDI (SMPTE ST 2082), 3G-SDI (SMPTE424M), HD-SDI (SMPTE292M), SD-SDI (SMPTE259M)
    User settings The function settings for up to 20 users can be stored.
    Color tone adjustment Adjust the color tone of each endoscopic image for White light observation mode, NBI observation mode, and RDI observation mode.
    • Red adjustment: ± 8 steps
    • Blue adjustment: ± 8 steps
    • Chroma adjustment: ± 8 steps
    Automatic gain control (AGC) The image can be electronically amplified when the light is inadequate due to the distal end of the endoscope being too fat from the object.
    Contrast • H (High): Darkens the dark part and brightens the bright part.
    • L (Low): Brightens the dark part and darkens the bright part.
    BAI-MAC Brightness adjustment with maintenance of contrast
    Iris The iris modes can be switched.
    • Auto: The brightness is adjusted based on the brightest part of the central part and the average brightness of the periphery part.
    • Peak: The brightness is adjusted based on the brightest part of the endoscopic image.
    • Average: The brightness is adjusted based on the average brightness of the endoscopic image.
    Image enhancement settings Fine patterns or edges in the endoscopic images can be enhanced electrically to increase the image sharpnes.
    • Enhancement type A: Emphasizes the pattern and contour of the endoscopic image.
    • Enhancement type B: Emphasizes the finer parts than structure emphasis type A.
    Switching the enhancement modes The enhancement level can be selected from 3 levels (OFF, 1, 2, and 3)
    Image size selection The size of the endoscopic image can be selected from 2 modes. (Except SDTV)
    Electric zoom Switch between mode 1, mode 2, and mode 3.
    PIP/POP Swktch brtween PIP and POP
    Aspect ratio Switch between 16:9 and 4:3. (Except SDTV)
    Freeze Freeze the endoscopic image.
    Pre-freeze The image with the least blur is selected from the images captured in the set time period before freeze operation and displayed
    Optical-digital observation The optical-digital observation can be performed. The endoscope compatible with the optical-digital observation is required.
    • NBI observation: This observation mode uses the narrow band light.
    • RDI observation: This observation mode uses the red dichromatic lights.
    • AFI observation: This observation mode uses the blue light.
    • TXI observation: This observation mode enhances color, texture and brightness.
    Beginning and ending examination Beginning and ending examination timing can be set interlock with the particular operation
    Custom switch Assign specific functions to the following buttons.
    • Remote switches (Up to 5)
    • Foot switches (Up to 2)
    • Keyboard custom key (Up to 4)
    • Touch panel custom button of basic functions screen (Up to 3)
    • Touch panel custom button of custom functions screen (Up to 10)
    MyCV mode Switch setting values of multiple functions at once
